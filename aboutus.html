About us

Derived by combining the “O” from ocean “S” from Sky and “erth” from Earth, comes a name that is easy to pronounce and read. Indicating a bigger scale of things.

Our Ocean

collection

Is inspired by the Ocean’s revitalizing properties. Adherence to the nature of water as it is always coming back in waves, giving your hair the balance and shine it needs to be healthy.

Our Sky

collection

Is inspired by the endless vastness of the sky and is for your hair to explore endless opportunities.

Our Earth

collection

Just as the earth is resilient, strong and robust. Our Earth collection is inspired by the earth to repair damaged hair, instilling the Earth’s strength in your hair, from root to tip.
